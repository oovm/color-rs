## Inputs

$$\begin{aligned}
r &∈ [0, 1]\\
g &∈ [0, 1]\\
b &∈ [0, 1]\\
a &∈ [0, 1]\\
\end{aligned}$$

## Outputs

$$\begin{aligned}
h &∈ [0°, 360°]\\
s &∈ [0\%, 100\%]\\
v &∈ [0\%, 100\%]\\
a &∈ [0\%, 100\%]\\
\end{aligned}$$

## Formula

$$\begin{aligned}
\rm{min} &= \min(r, g, b) \\
\rm{max} &= \max(r, g, b) \\
Δ &= \rm{max} - \rm{min} \\
\end{aligned}$$

$$ \begin{aligned}

h &= 60 ×\begin{cases}
0 & \text{if}\quad \Delta = 0 \\
\dfrac{g - b}{\Delta} \ \%\  6 & \text{if}\quad \rm{max} = r \\
\dfrac{b - r}{\Delta} + 2 & \text{if}\quad \rm{max} = g \\
\dfrac{r - g}{\Delta} + 4 & \text{if}\quad \rm{max} = b \\
\end{cases} \\

s &= 100 × \begin{cases}
0 & \text{if}\quad \rm{max} = 0 \\
\dfrac{\Delta}{\rm{max}} & \text{otherwise} \\
\end{cases} \\

v &= 100 × \rm{max} \\

\end{aligned}$$
